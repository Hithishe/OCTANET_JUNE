Karnataka

Chikkamagaluru

A picturesque town in Karnataka, India, is famed for its lush coffee plantations, serene hills, and breathtaking natural beauty.

Gokarna

A coastal town known for its pristine beaches, such as Om Beach and Kudle Beach, as well as its temples and laid-back atmosphere.

Mysore

Mysuru, the ‘City of Palaces’, is royalty and magnificence embodied; overflowing with history, sprawling gardens and tree-lined boulevards.
